This website provides simple booking options and it is user friendly and easy to use.

Q - How do I make a booking on Trainy.com?

A - You can book a ticket in five easy steps: Head over to the Trainy.com booking page, Enter your departure and arrival destinations, Select your travel dates, Choose from our wide range of trains based on your fare preferences, Click on book icon and your booking is done.

Q - Can I avail offers on Trainy.com?

A - Of course, you can. While making bookings, you can avail any special offer that is active at that time. In accordance with the offer selected, a listing of eligible trains would show up on your screen. You can then apply for the same and click on the downwards arrow, following which budget-friendly trains would start showing up.

Q - Why could I not avail the train booking offers at the time of checkout?

A - Trainy.com makes use of a real-time reservation database to list fare and availability. As dynamic changes in fare take place, or the available train tickets sell out, the database reflects the changes in real-time.

Q - How can I get low budget tickets on Trainy.com?

A - It’s super-easy to avail budget train fare while booking your tickets on Trainy.com. Just avail coupans and apply them durign payment form PayUmoney from Trainy.com, you can select the downward arrow, which will show the lowest fare at the top and continue downward in ascending order.
